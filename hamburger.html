Step 1: Prepartion

So the first steo to your prepartion is the proper ingredients for a hamburger. What you're going to want is good quality lean beef, onions, some nice thin sliced cheese, a teaspoon of soy sauce, an egg, some clove garlic, garlic powder, dried parsley, dried basil, dried oregano, crushed dried rosemary, and salt + pepper. In prepartion for the eating, you're going to want to get out as many plates + cups of whatever beverage you're going to be drinking. Once you all the ingredients and table garments you're ready for a burger.

Step 2: Cooking

The cooking process of a hamburger is quite easy. First you want to start off with heating the grill up to a high heat to kill all the bacteria and get a nice tender eating experience. Once you're done heating the oven you will want to all the prepped ingredients in a large bowl. Make sure all the ingredients are spread out evenly as you're going to form as many patties as you like in an equal matter. After making the patties you want to put them on the grill and proceed to cook each side for 5 minutes. This will kill all the basteria as well as getting a nice tender burger ready to eat.

Step 3: Serving + Presentation

After each of the patties are cooked to a nice perfection you're going to take each one of them off and put them onto the the nice buns that you had ready, it would be great if the patties were not cold. After putting them onto the bun hand out all the condiements and go crazy with whatever you want. After all of this you should have a nicely cooked hamburger, with any of the condiments you choose and you can choose any other side dishes you have to pair with your hamburger.
