These Are 3 of My Hobbies I Enjoy Doing

Video Games

Video games are one of the things in my life that takes up a large portion of my time, along with homework. What I enjoy about video games is the myriad of games there are to play. There is no shortage of games that will please you.
Some my personal favorites are rougelike games, which are, dungeon crawling games with permanent death. One of my favorites is a rougelike called Enter The Gungeon, a bullet hell dungeon crawling with punishing mechanics, that rewards you for playing good, and punishes poor play.
Another one of my favorite games is called Overwatch. This game is a first person shooter where you work together with 5 other players against 6 other players and attempt to complete the objective, depending upon the gamemode and map you are currently playing.
What makes video games so fun is the how you can basically get whatever you want out of a video game, with so many companies pumping out new games every year you will never run out of the fun which is video games.

Rubik's Cubes

Another hobby of mine that I enjoy participating in is solving Rubiks's cubes. I enjoy solving rubik's cubes because you can only continue to get faster, as you progress through solving the cubes it opens up challenges that you've never experienced.
Originally learning how to solve the rubik's cube was a frustrating thing, it took many days, heck weeks even, to get a better undersatnding of how the cube actually worked, but when you finally get to solve it for the first time, it's a beautiful thing.
Rubik's cubes have such a plethora of options on how you're going to solve it and finding the fasest method and one that fits you is the key to bringing you solve time down, and at the same time, it feels hella good when you solve a rubik's cube in front of a crowd.
There are many different kinds of rubik's cubes and this opens up the pain excrutiating process of learning the algorithms and getting you muscle memory to continally respond to that situation. Rubik's cubes are a fun, but very frustrating hobby, but once you invest the time, you never look back again..
